PG Policies

Booking Policy

Residents are required to book their stay in advance. Booking can be made through our online portal or by contacting the management office directly.

Check-In and Check-Out

Check-in time is at 2:00 PM and check-out time is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be accommodated upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation requests must be made at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled check-in time to avoid cancellation charges. Cancellations made within 48 hours will incur a cancellation fee equivalent to one night's stay.

Security and Safety

For the safety and security of all residents, visitors must be registered at the front desk. Residents are responsible for the security of their belongings. The management is not liable for any loss or damage.

House Rules

All residents are expected to adhere to the house rules including noise restrictions, cleanliness standards, and respectful behavior towards fellow residents and staff.

Maintenance Requests

Residents can submit maintenance requests through the online portal or by contacting the management office. Urgent requests will be addressed promptly.

Guest Policy

Residents are allowed to have guests over for a limited duration. However, residents must inform the management in advance and ensure that their guests adhere to the house rules.

Termination of Stay

The management reserves the right to terminate the stay of any resident who violates the policies or engages in disruptive behavior, without refund.

Feedback and Suggestions

We welcome feedback and suggestions from residents to improve our services. Residents can provide feedback through the online feedback form or by contacting the management directly.
